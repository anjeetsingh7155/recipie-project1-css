ABOUT THE DHABA

Welcome to Coder Da Dhaba, where tech meets taste! We're not your average eatery; we're a fusion
of culinary delight and coding culture. Imagine a place where the aroma of sizzling street food
mingles with the hum of keyboards and the clatter of coffee cups.

Founded in 2023 by a group of tech enthusiasts and culinary wizards, Coder Da Dhaba is designed to
be the ultimate haven for programmers, techies, and food lovers alike. Our menu is a blend of
traditional dhaba favorites with a tech twist—think “Debugging Dosas” and “Algorithmic Aloo Tikki.”

At Coder Da Dhaba, we believe that great ideas are born over great food. Our space is outfitted with
high-speed Wi-Fi, comfy seating, and plenty of power outlets to keep your devices charged and your
creativity flowing. Whether you’re working on your latest project, collaborating on a hackathon, or
just enjoying a well-deserved break, we’ve got you covered.

Join us at Coder Da Dhaba for a unique experience where innovation and flavor come together.
Because here, we’re not just coding— we’re creating a delicious revolution!
