PEDA

INGREDIENTS

  • Condensed Milk - 1 can (400 grams)
  • Ghee (clarified butter) - 2 tablespoons
  • Milk - 1/2 , Cardamom Powder - 1/2 teaspoon

RECIPIE

  1. In a pan, heat ghee over medium heat.Stir in the milk powder and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Stir in the milk powder and cook for 2-3 minutes. Stir in the milk powder and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Stir in cardamom powder, chopped nuts, and raisins. Let it cool slightly, then shape into small balls or discs.

MAGGIE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pack of Maggi noodles (usually 70g)
  • 1 ½ cups of wate
  • 1 ½ cups of wate

RECIPIE

  1. In a pan, bring 1 ½ cups of water to a boil.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the Maggi noodle cake.
    Sprinkle the Maggi tastemaker over the noodles. Stir gently to ensure the noodles are evenly coated.
  3. Let everything cook for another 2 minutes or until the noodles and optional ingredients are cooked to your liking.
